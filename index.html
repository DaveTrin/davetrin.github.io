Data visualization, or data viz, as you may have noticed, many people like to call it, is how we communicate numerical or quantitative information in a visual manner. To do this, we use things like charts, graphs, maps, and sometimes even pictures and iconography.

A Dashboard is a way of displaying various types of visual data in one place. Usually, a dashboard is intended to convey different, but ralated information in an easy-to-digest form. And oftentimes, this includes things like key performance indicators (KPI)s or other important business metrics that stakeholders need to see and understand at a glance.
